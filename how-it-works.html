How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create your account by providing basic information and completing the verification process.

2. Create or Join a League

Start your own league or join an existing one with friends. Set the buy-in amount and other league rules.

3. Build Your Team

Select players for your fantasy team based on their real-life performances and statistics.

4. Manage Your Team

Make transfers, set your lineup, and manage your team throughout the season to maximize points.

5. Compete & Win

Compete against other players in your league. Points are awarded based on real-life performances of selected players.

6. Secure Payouts

At the end of the season, smart contracts execute the payouts based on the league rules and standings.
